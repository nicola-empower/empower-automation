The Automation Roadmap

PHASE_1 // FOUNDATION
01

The Time Audit

Track your time for 3 days. Highlight every task that is repetitive, requires no creativity, and involves moving data. These are your targets.

02

Standardise the Process

You cannot automate chaos. Write down the exact steps for your target task (e.g., "Invoice Creation"). If the steps change every time, you need to fix the process first.

03

Centralise Your Data

Ensure your 'truth' lives in one place (e.g., a Master Google Sheet or CRM), not scattered across sticky notes and email inboxes.

PHASE_2 // IMPLEMENTATION
04

Automate "Input" (Forms)

Stop typing data manually. Use digital forms (Google Forms, Typeform) for client intake so data arrives structured and ready to use.

05

Notification Loops

Set up simple triggers. When a form is submitted, instantly email yourself or post to Slack. Stop checking your inbox every 5 minutes.

06

Document Generation

Implement a script to auto-fill templates (Invoices, Contracts, Welcome Packs) from your central data. This saves hours per week.

PHASE_3 // SCALING
07

Integration (The Bridge)

Connect your separate tools. Make your CRM talk to your Accounting software. Remove the human "copy-paste" bridge.

08

Client-Facing Automation

Allow clients to book their own appointments, receive automatic status updates, and access their own files without emailing you.

09

Reporting & Insights

Build a live dashboard. Visualise your data automatically so you can make decisions based on facts, not gut feeling.

10

Continuous Review

Automation is alive. Review your systems quarterly. As your business changes, your bots need new instructions.
