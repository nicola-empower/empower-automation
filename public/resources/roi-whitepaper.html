Why smart businesses stop hiring people to do robot work.

01 // Executive Summary

Most business owners look at a £30/hour employee and think a 1-hour task costs them £30. They are wrong.

When you factor in the distraction, the "switching cost" of changing focus, and the inevitable human errors, that task is likely costing you three times as much. This document breaks down the maths behind automation and proves why bespoke scripts – not expensive software subscriptions – are the highest-return investment you can make this year.

02 // The "3x Rule" of Errors

We recently upgraded our internal ROI calculators to include a new factor: The Human Error Rate. If you have a human copy-pasting data from an email to a spreadsheet 50 times a week, they will make a mistake. It is not if, it is when.

>> THE 3X RULE

Fixing a mistake takes, on average, 3x longer than doing the task correctly the first time. You have to find the error, investigate the cause, and repair the damage.

Automation does not get tired. It does not get distracted by a phone call. It copies the data perfectly, every single time, whether it is 9 AM or 3 AM.

03 // Case Study: The Invoice Bot

The Problem: A client was manually generating 20 invoices a week. It took 15 minutes per invoice. (Total: 5 hours/week).

These 5 hours were not just "time". They were 5 hours the business owner wasn't selling, leading, or resting.

The Solution: We wrote a simple script connecting their Google Sheet to a PDF generator template.

>> THE OUTCOME
  • Time reduced to 0 minutes (Automatic generation).
  • Errors reduced to 0%.
  • Annual Savings: £7,800 in billable time recovered.
