Automate Your Reports with Google Apps Script & Slides

By Nicola | August 2, 2025

As a tradesperson, freelancer, or small business owner, your time is your most valuable asset. You’re juggling everything; client work, quotes, invoices, scheduling, and keeping your business ticking over. The last thing you need is to get bogged down in repetitive administrative tasks. Constantly copying, pasting, and manually updating information can eat into your profit margins and leave you with less time for what you do best.

You’re likely thinking, “There has to be a smarter way to handle all this digital back-and-forth!” And you’d be absolutely right.

Allow me to introduce you to a powerful tool that can revolutionise how you manage your operations: Google Apps Script.

What Exactly Is Apps Script?

Put simply, Google Apps Script is a cloud-based tool that lets you automate tasks across your Google Workspace apps. Think of it as your personal digital assistant. You tell it what to do once, and it performs that task precisely and consistently every time. It’s like having an extra pair of hands that never gets tired. It works seamlessly within the Google apps you already use, such as Google Sheets, Google Docs and Google Slides.

How Can This Automation Benefit Your Business?

Let’s consider a common scenario for many small businesses and freelancers: You might need to send out regular job reports, client updates, or marketing presentations. This often involves:

  1. Creating a new document or presentation with today’s date and the client’s details.
  2. Pulling specific numbers (i.e., hours worked, materials used, or sales figures) from a spreadsheet.
  3. Adding a chart or graph to show progress or performance, also updated from your data.
  4. Sending the final document to your client or team.

Empower Your Business, Enhance Your Client Relationships

For tradespeople, freelancers, and small business owners, integrating Google Apps Script is a game-changer. It’s not just about saving a bit of time; it’s about making your business more efficient, reliable, and professional.

Boost Your Efficiency

Reclaim hours you spend on routine admin. This means more time for new projects, serving existing clients, or even just enjoying a well-deserved break!

Minimise Errors

Automated processes are consistent. They reduce the risk of manual errors in crucial documents like invoices or reports.

Deliver More Value

By automating the mundane, you can focus on the skilled work you get paid for, delivering higher-quality results and better client service.

Project Professionalism

Consistently delivering polished, accurate, and timely documents makes your business stand out and builds trust with your clients.

In essence, Google Apps Script helps you streamline the behind-the-scenes work, allowing you to concentrate on what truly drives your business forward. It’s about working smarter, not harder, and giving you back that precious time.
