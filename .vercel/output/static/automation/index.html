What exactly is Google Apps Script automation?
Google Apps Script is a cloud-based scripting language that allows me to build custom workflows and automations within Google Workspace apps like Sheets, Gmail, and Calendar. It helps businesses save time by automating repetitive manual tasks.
Do I need to be tech-savvy to use your automation solutions?
Not at all! I design every automation to be user-friendly and easy to maintain. I provide clear instructions, training, and ongoing support so you and your team can benefit without needing coding skills.
Which apps and platforms can you connect?
I work with Google Workspace apps plus popular tools like HubSpot, Mailchimp, Shopify, Stripe, Trello, Slack, Salesforce, WooCommerce, and many others that offer APIs for integration.
How long does it take to set up an automation?
Setup time varies based on complexity - simple automations can be ready in a few days, while more complex workflows may take a few weeks. I always provide an estimated timeline during our discovery call.
Will automation replace my current software or staff?
Automation is designed to complement your existing tools and team, not replace them. It frees up your people to focus on high-value work by handling tedious, repetitive tasks automatically.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Google Apps Script runs securely within Google’s environment. I follow best practices to ensure your data is protected, private, and compliant with regulations like GDPR.
