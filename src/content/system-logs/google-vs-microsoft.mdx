## What About Python?

While Apps Script is king for Google Workspace, Python is the master of everything else. It’s my tool of choice for more advanced tasks like web scraping for market research or API integrations to connect different software. The two work together beautifully: Python can gather the data, and Apps Script can automate what happens with that data inside your Google Sheets and Docs.
